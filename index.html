Case Studies

Heavy rains flooded solar farms causing damage to inverters and mounting structures. Resilience measures: raised mounting, waterproof enclosures and emergency disconnects.

Poor drainage reduced agricultural productivity. Interventions included improved drainage canals and community awareness on irrigation scheduling.

Storm surges increased soil salinity affecting crops. Solutions: salt-tolerant varieties, raised beds, and constructed wetlands to reduce salt intrusion.
